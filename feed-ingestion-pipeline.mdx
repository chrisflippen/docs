## Key

- Every row is attributed to a dealer before it lands. The domain guard fails open and blocks only on an explicit conflict.
- Resolved rows upsert into the vehicle store. Conflicting or unresolved rows are quarantined in the audit log, never dropped.
- Feeds are served from a 15-minute cache and rebuilt live on a miss. Merchant Center fetches on its own schedule.
- Price enrichment runs off a queue and writes scraped prices back to the store.
- Solid arrows are runtime flow; the dotted arrow is the optional AI fallback.
