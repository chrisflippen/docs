**Key**

- The cockpit is where your team works. It holds no database, no queue, and no Google credentials — everything runs on the backend, which you reach over a signed-in session.
- Inventory flows into the system, the pipeline turns it into a compliant vehicle-ad feed, and an enrichment worker fills in and improves listing details as it goes.
- Every change that writes to Google passes through a write-guard that defaults to DENY, so nothing is published unless explicitly allowed.
