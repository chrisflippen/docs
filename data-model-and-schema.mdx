**Key**

- `dealers` is the hub. Identity and ownership fan out to inventory, account links, and feed mappings.
- Relationship labels show on-delete behavior. **CASCADE** deletes children with the parent. **SET NULL** keeps the row and nulls the link so audit and attribution history survives.
- Feed sync and health hang off the linked account (`dealer_external_accounts`), not the dealer directly — health is a property of the account.
- This is the core spine, about a dozen of the ~27 tables. The queue, provisioning, and auth tables follow the same foreign-key conventions.
