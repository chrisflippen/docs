**Key**

- The wizard never acts in Google for you. Each step teaches, deep-links into the exact Google panel, then verifies the result.
- Every page load re-reads the latest saved state and re-derives the five step statuses. It makes no Google calls to render the page.
- "Check status" or any save re-reads and re-derives. That is the only way a step status changes.
- Publish is the one gated step. It needs a linked Merchant Center, then registers the data source — safe to repeat even if you click again.
