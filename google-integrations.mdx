## Key

- One service account authenticates every Google surface. There is no per-dealer sign-in.
- The account a change touches is determined by the dealer you are working in, never by the incoming request.
- Every Google write passes a guard that defaults to DENY. Reads, discovery, and feed generation never do.
- A merchant swap runs as one locked, all-or-nothing unit per dealer. A superseded link is finalized, never rolled back.
- Business Profile is linked through Merchant Center during provisioning, not as a separate surface.
