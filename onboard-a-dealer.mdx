**What you'll do**

- Confirm the dealer's identity and store profile
- Link their Google surfaces (Merchant Center is required to publish)
- Trigger feed ingestion
- Register the hosted feed with Merchant Center

## Before you start

Make sure these are in place:

- The dealer record exists. Create it from the new-dealer page if it doesn't.
- The dealer's inventory is flowing into the system. Ingestion runs automatically once inventory is arriving.

## The five steps

Open the dealer's setup wizard. The wizard is resumable; it always lands you on the earliest step that needs attention.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identity">
    Read-only. Confirm the dealer name and Merchant Center account ID look right. This step is always complete once the dealer record exists.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Store profile">
    Enter the dealer's website, confirm their additional-fees policy, and fill in the full business address (street, city, state, ZIP). Add the Google Business Profile store code here if you have it — you'll need it before publish.

    **Success:** all four required fields saved (website, fees confirmed, street address, city/state/ZIP).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect">
    Link Google surfaces. Each card walks you through what to do and gives you a deep-link into the relevant Google console.

    - Merchant Center is required to reach the publish step.
    - Google Ads, GA4, and Search Console are optional but recommended.

    **Success:** at least one surface linked; Merchant Center linked specifically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Feed">
    Request an inventory feed from the provider, or confirm inventory is already flowing into the system. The wizard shows a live inventory count.

    **Success:** feed request marked "received" or live inventory count is above zero.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Connect generated feed**. This registers the hosted feed with Merchant Center — idempotent, so it's safe to re-run.

    This step unlocks as soon as Merchant Center is linked. To actually connect the feed you also need the Google Business Profile store code set — without it the Connect button stays disabled and shows a "Store code required" notice pointing back to Step 2.

    **Success:** the feed data source appears in Merchant Center and the wizard shows this step as complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After publish

Merchant Center fetches the feed on its own schedule. To confirm it worked sooner:

1. Open Merchant Center → Products → Feeds and trigger a manual fetch.
2. Verify the data source appears and isn't in a taken-down state.
3. Confirm Vehicle Ads or Free Listings destinations show as active.

## If something's off

| Symptom | Fix |
|---|---|
| Publish step is locked | Link Merchant Center in Step 3 |
| "Store code required" callout | Go back to Step 2 and add the Google Business Profile store code |
| Publish button does nothing | Confirm publishing is enabled for your account and you have permission to publish, then retry |
| Merchant Center can't fetch the feed | Re-run the Publish step (it's idempotent) to re-register the data source, then trigger a manual fetch in Merchant Center |
| Inventory count stuck at zero | Confirm inventory is flowing into the system; ingestion runs automatically once it arrives. If the count stays at zero, check with the inventory provider |
